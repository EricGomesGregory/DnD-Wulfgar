## The Abandoned Castle
The party is tasked by Franz Lange Himmelsspeer Castle, a keep located half a mile away from Melfia that has been abandoned for the last ten years after a incident that caused all its residents to disappear in the middle of the night.

In reality the keep was the sight of a sacrifice, with its denizens serving as kindling for the keep's lord to ascend into an eldritch creature that now lives within the keep.

___
The party begins their adventure within the city of Melfia where they can search for clues by either passing a DC 20 Wisdom (Perception) check or Charisma (Deception / Persuasion) checks to overhear or coax information from the general population. Or alternatively they can search the city records of the event with a DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check.

##### Truth
The known truths about the incident are:
- The lord of the keep was involved with a group of thieves that were operating during that night (*Int*)
- The lord was an exocentric shodden (*Wis*, *Cha*)
- The household was small and devoid of hairs (*Int*, *Cha*)

##### Lies
|  d4 | Rumors on the Street                         | Dubious Report                                   |
|:---:|:---------------------------------------------|:-------------------------------------------------|
|  1  | The residents were killed by the "dragons"   | The keep was infiltrated by an assassin          |
| 2/3 | They vanished to escape their debt           | The residents were spirited away                 |
|  4  | The lord was a werewolf that killed them all | The lord was a psychopath that poisoned the keep |

___
After investigating the events of the night the party must then reach and enter the abandoned keep.
