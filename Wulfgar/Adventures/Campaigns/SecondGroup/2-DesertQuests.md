## Quests in Shidai


### Treasure Hunt
Jabedhaia has acquired a treasure map, that according to his informant, are the ruins of an ancient adernian palace. He explains to the party that the items of the palace can be sold to restructure the Nasir's estate.

A character proficient in **cartographer's tools** can spend 1 hour to determine a path to the ruins.


#### Voyage to the Ruins
If the party has determined the path to the ruins previously a character must make a Wisdom (Survival) check. If the party haven't determined the path the Wisdom (Survival) check is made with disadvantage.

##### Voyage Encounters
| Survival | Encounter                      |
|:-----:|:----------------------------------|
|   1   | A mirage of an oasis              |
|  2-7  | 1d4 **bandits**                   |
|  8-13 | An abandoned camp with old tools  |
| 14-19 | 2d4 **scorpions**                 |
|  20+  | An oasis surrounded by palm trees |

#### The Ruins
The party reaches a rocky are where the dunes reveal a small house with faded blue pillars and walls decorated with sun bleached paintings of dogs and birds. The main entrance is a large doorway that has partially collapsed with the weight of the sands.

#### Dining Room
From the main corridor the party can enter one of two doors into a large, partially collapsed, room. Within it they find a stone table in the center, broken wooden chairs and broken plates on the ground.

On a successful Intelligence (Investigation) or Wisdom (Perception) check the party can find the following items based on the roll.

- *10-14.*  A silver cup with traces of beer.

- *15-19.*  A piece of parchment with a strange symbol (DC 20 Intelligence (Arcana) check) to determine its an arcane symbol.

- *20+.*  Traces of red cloth die.

#### Kitchen
From the main corridor the party can enter a door into a room covered in mold with broken tiles on the ground and water sprouting from the corner. This once was a kitchen but now is a broken room.

On a successful Intelligence (Investigation) or Wisdom (Perception) check the party can find the following items based on the roll.

- *10-14.*  A set of fine silverware.

- *15-20+.*  A golden ring inscribed with the text *My one true love, my ever blooming flower*.

#### Living Room
The living room is a completely intact room with eight pillars holding the roof. The walls are adorned with chipped paintings of satyrs dancing in the grass, The pillars are adorned with paintings of roses and the ground has a large circle with a geometric pattern inscribed within.

On a DC 20  Intelligence (Investigation) or Wisdom (Perception) check a character can determine that there's a room under the circle in the center of the living room. A character proficient in Arcana can determine that the circle is a vault door that can only be opened by attempting a spellcasting (DC 10).

On a successful spellcasting check the center of the circle opens to reveal a hidden room with a study room containing table with a robed skeleton lying on a book with a red died linen cover. The party can find the following items:
___
***Map.***
a rough map of the region with an X marked near Carthus with a the text *Dig sight*. 
___
***Vile of Red Liquid.***
The contents of the vile smell reinvigorating. It's a Potion Of Healing (2d4 + 2).
___
***Golden Ring.***
A golden ring with a ruby embossed in it and inscribed with the text *My one true love, my alchemist*.
___
***Red Alchemist's Book.***
A large tome with a cover made of red linen. In the interior of the book is composed of strange drawings depicting the same seven characters: a queen with red hair, a king with golden hair, two bishops dressed in blue, and three woman dressed in green and caring tree branches; performing mondaine acts such as entering or exiting a castle.

With a successful DC 20 Intelligence (Investigation) check of proficiency with Alchemist's supplies a character can discern that the book is encoded with the recipe for **Healing Potions**.

#### Bedroom
A room with walls painted with drawings of the sea and boats. In the center of the room the party can find a small bed with dusty blankets. This once was the room of a small child.


#### Main Bedroom

### Stealing it Back
The inquisition of the Holy Path has arrested a noble on the charges of being a cultist of a dark god and a mage and his collection of artifacts are being held in a shipyard on the banks of the Nhar river.
___
As the day begins, a massive crowd congregates around the TEMPLE_NAME cathedral to witness the execution of a noble on the charges of occultism and witchcraft. The noble in question was a friend of the Nasir family.

Jabedhaia informs the party that some of the nobles artifacts, acquired by Saladin's parents, are being held in a shipyard on the banks of the Nhar river and those items will be destroyed and dumped in the river in a ceremony by the Holy Path.

He then informs the party that they should steal back some of the artifacts and re sell them in the black market so they can fund future expeditions.

#### Preparations
Jabedhaia helps the party by giving them a map of the shipyard and organizing a meeting with a smuggler named Kidane

___
***Kidane.***
Kidane is a 5'8'' young man with long black braided hair, a muscular body, dark skin, and brown eyes. He wears a green robe with white birds adorning it.

He is a simple man working to make ends meet but he owes Jabedhaia a favor. He is not a fighter but he is a great mariner skilled in navigating through the canals of the various cities along the Nhar river.
