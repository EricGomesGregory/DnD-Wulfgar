> ## Warebar
>*Medium humanoid(any, shapechanger), neutral evil*
> ___
> - **Armor Class** 18 (Natural Armor)
> - **Hit Points** 18 (4d8)
> - **Speed** 20 ft., fly 30 ft.
>___
>|   STR   |   DEX   |   CON   |   INT   |   WIS   |   CHA   |
>|:-------:|:-------:|:-------:|:-------:|:-------:|:-------:|
>|  8 (-1) | 20 (+5) | 10 (+0) | 11 (+0) | 20 (+5) |  8 (-1) |
>___
> - **Saving Throws** Dex +7
> - **Skills** Perception +7, Survival +7
> - **Damage Resistances** Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from non-magical attacks.
> - **Condition Immunities** NONE
> - **Senses** Blindsight 120 ft., Darkvision 60 ft., passive Perception 17
> - **Languages** LANGUAGE
> - **Level** 4 **PB** +2
> ___
> ***Keen Hearing.***
> The warebat has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing.
>
> ***Ecolocation.***
> Creatures can't attempt to hide if they are within the Blindsight range of the warebat, regardless of line of sight. If the warebat is Deafened it loses its Blindsight.
>
> ***Frightened Scream.***
> The warebat utters a blood curling scream. Each creature within 60 feet of the warebat must succeed on a DC 00 Wisdom saving throw or be Frightened for 1 minute. A Frightened creature can repeat the saving throw at the stat of each of its turn.
>
>
> ### Actions
> ***Multiattack.*** The warebat makes two claw attacks.
>
> ***Bite.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit* 10 (4d4) piercing damage. If the creature is a humanoid it must succeed on a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or be Cursed with warebat lycanthropy. 
>
> ***Claw.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +7 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit* 7 (1d4 + 5) slashing damage. 
>
